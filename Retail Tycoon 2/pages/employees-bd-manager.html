Managers

What do they do?

A manager is an employee you can hire to work in your store.

They order items to sell in your store.
You can choose whether or not they order with instant delivery and their amount to stock.

Each time they level up, their spending limit goes up by 20% (level 5: 80% higher spending limit).

Amount to stock

The amount to stock option tells your manager how much stock they need to buy:
造 50%: Your manager will order as much items that are needed to fill all your shelves half full.
造 100%: Your manager will order as much items to fill all your shelves fully.
造 150%: Your manager will order as much items to fill all your shelves fully + have as much items in storage to fill all your shelves for 50% again.
造 200%: Your manager will order as much items to be able to fill all your shelves twice.

Having multiple managers set to 200% won't make them stock to 200%, they will work together to reach the goal of 200%.
