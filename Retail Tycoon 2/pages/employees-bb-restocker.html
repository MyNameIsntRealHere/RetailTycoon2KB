Restockers

What do they do?

A restocker is an employee you can hire to work in your store.

They fill your shelves with the items you have in storage (they wont put random items on random shelves, you will need to assign items to specific shelves via the restocking menu at the top of your screen).
Each time they level up, they walk 15% faster (level 5: 60% faster).
